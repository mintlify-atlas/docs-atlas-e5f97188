## What is RTO Profit Simulator?

RTO Profit Simulator is a powerful, interactive web application designed to help e-commerce businesses calculate and visualize the financial impact of Return to Origin (RTO) losses on their bottom line. Built specifically for businesses operating with Cash on Delivery (COD) payment models, this tool provides real-time insights into how RTO rates affect profitability and offers actionable strategies for improvement.

## Understanding the RTO problem

Return to Origin (RTO) occurs when a COD order is returned undelivered to the seller. This happens due to various reasons:

- Customer refuses to accept the order
- Incorrect address or unreachable customer
- Customer unavailable during delivery attempts
- Fraudulent or fake orders

Each RTO order creates a triple loss:

1. **Forward shipping cost** - Money spent to ship the product to the customer
2. **Return shipping cost** - Additional cost to ship the product back
3. **Product cost** - Inventory blocked and potential product damage

For businesses with high COD percentages and RTO rates above 20%, these losses can eliminate profitability entirely.

## Why RTO matters

Consider a typical e-commerce business:

- 10,000 monthly orders
- 60% COD orders (6,000 orders)
- 30% RTO rate (1,800 failed deliveries)
- ₹500 product cost + ₹60 forward + ₹60 return shipping

**Monthly RTO loss: ₹11,16,000** (₹1.3+ crore annually)

If this business reduces RTO by just 10 percentage points (30% → 20%), they could save **₹3.72 lakh monthly** or **₹44.6 lakh annually** - without increasing sales.

This simulator helps you identify these opportunities in your business.
