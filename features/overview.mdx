The RTO Profit Simulator provides a powerful set of features to help e-commerce businesses understand and reduce their Cash on Delivery (COD) return losses. Each feature is designed to provide actionable insights that directly impact your bottom line.

## Core Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Business Inputs" icon="sliders" href="/features/business-inputs">
    Configure your business parameters including order volume, pricing, shipping costs, and RTO rates to calculate accurate financial impact.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Metrics Display" icon="chart-line" href="/features/metrics-display">
    View real-time KPIs including total revenue, RTO losses, realized revenue, net profit, and break-even RTO percentage.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="RTO Simulator" icon="gauge" href="/features/rto-simulator">
    Interactive slider to simulate profit improvements by reducing your RTO percentage and see potential savings instantly.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Visual Analytics" icon="chart-bar" href="/features/visual-analytics">
    Interactive charts showing financial composition and the impact of RTO reduction on your profitability.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="PDF Export" icon="file-arrow-down" href="/features/pdf-export">
    Export comprehensive reports with all business inputs, calculated metrics, and optimization projections.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Additional Insights

Beyond the core calculator features, the simulator includes:

- **AI-Powered Insights**: Contextual recommendations based on your RTO rate, COD percentage, and break-even thresholds
- **Risk Meter**: Visual gauge showing your RTO risk level with actionable advice
- **Prepaid Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of your current scenario vs. a 100% prepaid model
- **Monthly/Annual Views**: Toggle between monthly and annual projections for better planning

<Info>
  All calculations update in real-time as you adjust your business inputs, providing immediate feedback on how changes impact your profitability.
</Info>

## Getting Started

Start by configuring your [Business Inputs](/features/business-inputs) to match your current operations, then explore the various analytics and simulation tools to identify optimization opportunities.
